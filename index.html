about me

Hello, I'm Roz.

Welcome to my portfolio website. This is a space for me to showcase my coding and design work.

I am at the start of my web development journey, having decided to change careers after several jobs left me feeling uninspired. I've always had an interest in visual design, and love problem-solving, so web development is an area I feel really passionate about.

So far I have worked with Javascript/Node.js, Express.js, PostgreSQL, Knex, HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap and Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop. I use Git and GitHub for version control and employ agile practices and TDD as much as possible.

I am always keen to learn new technologies and regularly follow tutorials on Udemy and YouTube to keep my learning up-to-date.

I hope you enjoy looking at my work as much as I did creating it!
